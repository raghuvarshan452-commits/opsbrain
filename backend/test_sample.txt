Maintenance Inspection Report

Site: Chennai Manufacturing Plant, Unit 3
Report Date: March 15, 2026
Prepared by: Rajesh Kumar, Senior Maintenance Engineer

Summary:
On March 15, 2026, a scheduled inspection was carried out on Equipment P-204 (Hydraulic Press Unit) located in Bay 7. The inspection was conducted by Rajesh Kumar and reviewed by Priya Sharma, Compliance Officer.

Findings:
Equipment P-204 showed signs of pressure irregularity during the test cycle. Vibration levels exceeded the threshold specified in Standard IS 800:2007 for structural steel components. A follow-up inspection is scheduled for April 2, 2026.

Equipment T-118 (Conveyor Motor Assembly) was also inspected on the same date and found to be operating within normal parameters, compliant with ISO 9001:2015 quality requirements.

Personnel Involved:
- Rajesh Kumar (Senior Maintenance Engineer) — lead inspector
- Priya Sharma (Compliance Officer) — sign-off authority
- Arjun Nair (Safety Coordinator) — safety clearance

Recommendations:
Equipment P-204 requires a replacement hydraulic seal before April 10, 2026, per manufacturer guidelines under Standard IS 4247:1992. Equipment T-118 is cleared for continued operation with no immediate action required.

Next scheduled audit: May 1, 2026.
